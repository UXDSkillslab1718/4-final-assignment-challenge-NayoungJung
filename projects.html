UXD School Projects

Prgramming Bootcamp

Nov 2, 2018 - Nov 9, 2018


How can programming generate artwork?
During the week of Programming Bootcamp, five artworks were created by using Processing, a programming development tool. The artworks contained distinctive topics. Additional libraries, such as audio and video, were used to allow people to interact with my artworks.

Interaction Design

Nov 12, 2018 – Jan 25, 2019


The aim of this project was to learn the basic notion of interactive design and its application for a website. At the same time, my team had to take the need of the client into account. As a result, a clickable prototype, wireframes, for a movie theater company was created.

Research for Design 2

Nov 13, 2018 – Jan 21, 2019


Throughout the course, I gained knowledge about what ethnographic design is and how to synthesize collected data. The general topic of the research was ‘the main meal’, and my personal topic was ‘how students share a communal place with other people’. Various fieldwork experiences were conducted, and a suggestion towards the topic was drawn out.
